Fullscreen Web App Setup

🍎 iPhone / iPad (iOS Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button (box with an upward arrow) in the Safari toolbar.
  2. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Give it a name and tap Add in the top-right corner.
  4. Launch Pi Pad from your phone's home screen. It will open in a clean, standalone full-screen window with no address bar!

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the Menu icon (three vertical dots) in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
  3. Open the newly installed app from your home screen to enjoy the borderless full-screen experience.
